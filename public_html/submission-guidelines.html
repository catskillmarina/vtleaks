Currently we are in the process of setting up the infrastructure for recieving anonymous tips. Once the infrastructure is operational we request you follow the following submission guidelines.



  • submissions should be of materials not already publicly available or that have been suppressed in some way.
  • include a description of the materials and their relevance
  • organize the material if possible - zip, tar or 7z archives are appreciated
  • do not submitt allegations without documentary proof. If you are reporting on something you witnessed please include a way for us or our media partners to verify what you are reporting.
  • Be aware that formats like PDF, word processor documents, images and video may contain metadata that can be used to identify you. If you can, scrub metadata. We will do our best to clean documents we recieve however it is best that we cannot identify you at all.
