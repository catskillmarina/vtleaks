Submitting Tips

  • Go to https://torproject.org
    Click on download tor.
  • The website will attempt to direct you to the proper version of torbrowser for your computer. If it does not guess correctly - click on view all downloads.
  • Follow the instructions for the operating system your computer is running and start the tor browser. If all has gone properly you will see something like the following.
  • Once you have connected to tor, go to the VTLeaks submission URL at http://lieiqr4um6cdgo2c.onion. You will only be able to view and use this site using the tor browser or directly through the tor network.
  • The process of submitting and viewing tips is described in the following set of screenshots.
  • Choose your receiver. This is the entity that is to receive your submission.
  • Enter brief information regarding your submission and upload any relevant files.
