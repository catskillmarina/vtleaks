VTleaks was founded on March 10th 2014 to promote transparency and accountability in Vermont government, finances, corporations, law enforcement , jails and prisons. We are hosting a GLobaLeaks instance on the TOR anonymity network to allow whistleblowers to submit news and documents that will be passed on to Vermont media. We are actively seeking journalists, legal minds and data transparency activists to form a core group.
